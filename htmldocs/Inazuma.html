Inazuma Sceneries

Grand Narukami Shrine

"The Grand Narukami Shrine is located at the peak of Mt. Yougou and is the largest shrine in Inazuma, guarding the Sacred Sakura. It is the largest shrine in Inazuma and provides much-needed comfort and peace to the people of Inazuma in these unsettling times." - Genshin Impact

This shrine has several cherry blossom trees that have falling leaves. Rare Sakura blooms are present here in this place. In night, you can see the glowing lanterns on the shrine that make the place more beautiful and aesthetic.

Asase Shrine

Asase shrine is located at Serai Island. The shrine was made to vanquish the lingering power of Kanna Kapatcir, a.k.a. the Thunderbird. Just like the Grand Narukami Shrine, this place also has several pink cherry blossom trees. Aside from that, this shrine is also know for its adorable cats around the place. One of them is Neko who is a black cat sitting on the counter of the shrine and a non-playable character that can talk.

Yashiori Island

"The skeleton of a great snake rests on this island. The presence of evil is palpable here." - Genshin Impact

The island is known for the skeletal head of Orobashi, the giant serpent. As you explore the place, there are also remained bones of Orobashi where the Crystal Marrows are produced from. There are maple trees with red leaves around this place as one of Inazuma's landmarks.
