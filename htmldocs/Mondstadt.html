Mondstadt Sceneries

Mondstadt City

"The wind will carry Dandelion Seeds, songs, and stories far afield, and it also guides gentle Travelers to its realm. Welcome to Mondstadt." - Genshin Impact

The city of Mondstadt welcomes all people with open hands according to the Knights of Favonius handbook. The place features stony streets, German-style houses, and towering windmills in the lower area. You can see several knights, merchants, and citizens walking around. As you go up, there is a headquarters for Favonius knights, a cathedral, a giant statue of the Anemo archon. This place serves as the in-game home for many players because of its cozy and gentle atmosphere.

Starsnatch Cliff

Starsnatch Cliff is a subarea located in the east part of Starfell Valley. From here, you can see Mondstadt City, as well as its neighboring places. You can also view the horizon that meets the sea and the sky from this cliff. Starsnatch Cliff is the only home to the rare Cecilia flowers.

Windrise

"This plain bathes in an everlasting breeze. An ancient, monumental tree left behind by an ancient heroine speaks softly amidst the winds." - Genshin Impact

Windrise is known for its giant, ancient tree with its green foliage. There is also a vast and spacious land surrounding the tree where you can roam around. Several crystalflies can be seen near the tree.
