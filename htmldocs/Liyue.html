Liyue Sceneries

Liyue Harbor

"A prosperous port city where a thousand ships drop anchor. Under the aegis of countless contracts and regulations, innumerable goods change hands and grace the seven nations with their presence." - Genshin Impact

This harbor is the main city of Liyue. And, as the major landmark of this nation, this is where most of the trades, businesses, agreements, and other economic activities are being done. The buildings here is inspired by the traditional chinese architectures. As you explore this place, there are plenty of mileliths patrolling around and merchants that you can talk with. This is also where the Lantern Rite event takes place with the wonderful decorations all over the harbor.

Qingyun Peak

High above the peaks of the towering mountains of Minlin, there lies a small island-like place that floats like a cloud. This location is unlocked after completing the Three Cranes Puzzle. From here, you can see the clouds that covers everything below it. You can also perceive the beautiful orange sunset at this place.

Qingce Village

"Mighty Mt. Qingce gently cradles this peaceful village, silently protecting the old, the young, and the abundant croplands within its embrace. The legend of the ancient threat subdued beneath the mountain has been consigned to legend along with those who know of it, never again to return." - Genshin Impact

This place features terraced fields of orange and yellow flowers that somehow resembles groups of fallen autumn leaves.
