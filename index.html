Mondstadt Sceneries

"A city of freedom that lies in the northeast of Teyvat. From amongst mountains and wide-open plains, carefree breezes carry the scent of dandelions — a gift from the Anemo God, Barbatos — across Cider Lake to Mondstadt, which sits on an island in the middle of the lake." - Genshin Impact

Mondstadt is one of the seven regions in Teyvat and is the first nation to be visited by the Traveler. It is known as the City of Freedom wherein the Anemo archon, Barbatos, rules the land. Dandelions, wine, Windwheel asters, Cecilia, Wolfberries, and Valberries are some of the local specialties in Mondstadt. Explore the nation by clicking the link/title above!

Liyue Sceneries

"A bountiful harbor that lies in the east of Teyvat. Mountains stand tall and proud alongside the stoneforest, that, together with the open plains and lively rivers, make up Liyue's bountiful landscape, which shows its unique beauty through each of the four seasons. Just how many gifts from the Geo God lie in wait amongst the rocks of Liyue's mountains?" - Genshin Impact

Liyue is one of the seven regions in Teyvat and is the second nation to be discovered by the Traveler. People there worships the Geo archon, Morax, and the nation is known as the City of Contracts. Liyue is also known for its rich minerals, such as Notilucous Jade and Cor Lapis, and other things, like Jueyun Chili, Glaze Lily, and Qingxin. Explore the nation by clicking the link/title above!

Inazuma Sceneries

"An Isolated Archipelago Far East of Teyvat Overcome endless thunderstorms and set foot on the islands of red maple and cherry blossoms. On winding shores and towering cliffs, and in forests and mountains full of secrets, witness the Eternity pursued by Her Excellency, the Almighty Narukami Ogosho." - Genshin Impact

Far from the lands of Mondstadt and Liyue lies the land of Inazuma. It is one of the seven regions in Teyvat and is the third nation to be discovered by the Traveler. Raiden Shogun, the current Electro Archon, governs the archipelago, and the nation is known as the City of Eternity. Despite its electrifying atmosphere, Naku weeds, Sakura blooms, and Crystal marrow are produced in this mysterious land. Explore the nation by clicking the link/title above!
